This Privacy Policy describes how Kaizoku-ō GFX ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "App").

By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

B. Information collected automatically

We do not sell or rent your personal information.
