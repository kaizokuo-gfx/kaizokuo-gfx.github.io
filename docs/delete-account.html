Kaizoku-ō GFX • Effective date: December 14, 2025

1) Account Deletion Requests

If you would like to delete your Kaizoku-ō GFX account (or request deletion of data linked to your use of the App), you can submit a deletion request by email.

If the App does not create an account for you, this page still applies as a data deletion request.

2) How to Request Deletion

Send us an email

  • To: dev.kaizokuo@gmail.com
  • Subject: Delete Account Request

Include the following in your message

  • The email/identifier you used in the app (if any).
  • Your device model and Android version.
  • Your app version (optional).
  • Any extra details that help identify your data (optional).

For safety, we may ask for additional verification to prevent unauthorized deletion requests.

Quick link: Email deletion request

3) What Will Be Deleted

Depending on what the App stores or uses, we may delete:

  • Account-related records (if accounts exist).
  • Identifiers linked to analytics and crash records where feasible.
  • Support conversation history (upon request), unless legally required to retain it.

Some information may be retained if required by law, security, fraud prevention, or to comply with platform obligations.

4) Timeline

We will review and process valid deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe. Complex cases may take longer.
